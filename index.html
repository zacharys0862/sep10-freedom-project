Car Technology

Cars have been around 1886 so we have had cars for almost a century and a half. Ever since the first car there has constantly been new innovations. From electric cars to computer chips inside of cars theres always been something brand new to improve on cars. This website will show off some of those innations and even addresses a problem that still exists today with my own innovaiton.

Innovations

MaxxECU

MaxxECU is a car tuning software that connects to a usb that connects to your car's computer to change certain things about the car. They have different types of plugin ECUs for different types of car models.

Tesla Model S Plaid Touchscreen

Touchscreens in cars are not something by a specific brand but they have been evolving as time goes on. Like in the Tesla Model S Plaid that has literally all of its controls on the center screen. It controls the whole car. Things that would usually be acessable through buttons on the car like the air conditioning and windshield wipers.

Tesla Keycard

Tesla has a card that will open your car for you. It works by tapping the card on the side of your car.

Rear Axle steering

There are some cars that have rear axle steering so that it can make sharper turns. This works by making the rear wheels turn in the opposite direction of what the front wheels are turning.

The camless engine or “Freevalve” is a way that a car can have more power as well as less pollution. This engine uses electronically controlled vavles and has more MPG.
